About JOCHEVED

JOCHEVED is a women's fashion boutique born with a clear purpose: to offer superior quality pieces that enhance the beauty and personality of every woman.

With over 15 years of experience in the fashion market, we have built our reputation through dedication to excellence, innovation and personalized service. Every piece in our catalog is carefully selected or produced with the finest materials available.

We believe fashion is a form of personal expression. That is why we work to offer not just clothing, but style solutions that respect the individuality of our clients.

Our mission is simple: to make every woman feel special, confident and beautiful when wearing our clothes. This means impeccable quality, timeless design and a genuine commitment to each customer's satisfaction.

Our Story

2009

The Origin of JOCHEVED

It all began with a simple vision: to create a space where women could find clothes that make them feel good. The first store opened in São Paulo with a small catalog of carefully selected pieces.

2012

Expansion and Recognition

With growing sales and customer satisfaction, we expanded our catalog and opened new locations across the city of São Paulo.

2015

The Digital Era

We launched our online platform, allowing customers from all over Brazil to access our collections. Technology enabled us to reach an even wider audience.

2018

Our Own Lines

We began developing our own collections, combining the expertise we had acquired with the personal vision of our designers.

2021

Sustainability

We implemented more sustainable practices across our operations, reflecting our responsibility towards the future and the planet.

2024

Present and Future

We continue to grow with the trust of over 10,000 satisfied customers, constantly innovating to offer the best shopping experience.
